Welcome to the text enrichment site to accompany Acting on Words: An Integrated Reader, Rhetoric, and Handbook, Second Edition by Brundage and Lahey.

This site has been created to complement Acting on Words, and features links to Athabasca University’s English-Language Self-Assessment Test and ESL support materials, as well as providing additional language support for selected readings, grammar and punctuation quizzes, sample essays, and instruction on additional types of writing not covered in the text.

We hope that this material will enhance Acting on Words for students and instructors alike.
