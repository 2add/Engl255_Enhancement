Click on the following link to take Athabasca University's English Language Self-Assessment Test:

http://www.athabascau.ca/html/services/counselling/esl/

This multi-purpose test is intended for students with English as a second language, as well as for those students who feel their English skills may need some improvement.

Your test score and results will be diagnosed automatically. You will receive a score out of 115, along with a recommendation of the level of course that is most suitable for your current skill base.

The test is free and should take no more than two hours to complete.
